Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026
In short: the data you enter in Sprkt (bikes, components, maintenance, photos) is stored only on your phone. We don't have servers, we don't create accounts, and we don't sell or share your data with anyone.

1. Who we are

Sprkt is a bike maintenance tracking app, developed independently. For questions about this policy, contact privacy@sprkt.app.

2. What data the app handles

Sprkt stores locally, on your device, the data you enter:

  • Information about your bikes (brand, model, type, mileage, purchase value)
  • Components and their wear history
  • Maintenance records (date, cost, shop, notes)
  • Photos you add to your bikes, if you choose to take or select one
  • Your app preferences (language, units, theme, currency)

This data never leaves your device, except when you choose to export it (see section 4).

3. What the app does not do

  • We don't require account creation or login
  • We don't collect personally identifiable information
  • We don't send your data to any server, ours or third-party
  • We don't sell or share your data with advertisers or other companies
  • We don't use third-party tracking or advertising tools

4. Backup and data export

Sprkt lets you export a backup file containing your data, so you can store it wherever you like (for example, your phone's personal cloud storage). This file is created and controlled entirely by you — Sprkt has no access to it once exported.

5. Device permissions

The app may request camera and photo library access only when you choose to add a photo to a bike. We only access the specific photo you select — we don't have broad access to your gallery.

6. Usage statistics

We may view aggregated, anonymous statistics provided by Google Play (such as install counts), which don't identify individual users or the content of their data.

7. Your rights

Since all data stays on your device, you have full control over it: you can edit it, export it, or delete it at any time directly in the app, or by uninstalling the app.

8. Children

Sprkt is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the date at the top of this page will be updated. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.

10. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@sprkt.app
For general support: support@sprkt.app
